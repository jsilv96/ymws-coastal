Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

At Oliva & Mare, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our restaurant, website, or use our online ordering service.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information when you interact with us:

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Reservation details, including date, party size, and any dietary or access requirements
  • Order information including items purchased and delivery preferences
  • Payment information processed through our secure third-party processors
  • Website usage data, cookies, and device information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process reservations and online orders
  • Communicate about your bookings, orders, or inquiries
  • Send marketing communications, only where you have opted in
  • Improve our offerings, service quality, and website experience
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal information. We only share your data with trusted service providers (payment processors, reservation platforms, delivery partners) under strict contractual safeguards, or when required by law.

Data Security

We maintain industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, restricted access, and regular security audits. However, no system is entirely immune, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@olivamare.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes implies acceptance.
