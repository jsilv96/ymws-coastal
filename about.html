About Oliva & Mare

Est. MMXIV

Where the olive grove meets the open water.

Oliva & Mare was born in 2014 on a quiet stretch of coastline north of Half Moon Bay. Chef Elena and her husband Petros crossed the Atlantic with a single ambition — to serve the Mediterranean the way they remembered it from their summers in Naxos.

Every element of our kitchen is an homage: the olive oil cold-pressed from Elena's family grove on the island, the flour milled in small batches from heritage grains, the sea bass delivered daily from sustainable coastal fisheries. Our wine list reads like a letter home — biodynamic Assyrtikos, volcanic reds from Santorini, amphora-aged whites from the Peloponnese.

More than a restaurant, Oliva & Mare is a practice. A reminder that the simplest ingredients — good oil, ripe tomato, fresh fish, open flame, kind company — are still the most profound.
