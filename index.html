About the House

All About Oliva & Mare

From the island of Naxos to our kitchen — we honor the slow rhythm of Mediterranean cooking. Each plate begins with sun-ripened produce, line-caught fish, and olive oil pressed from family groves. Dinner, the way it was meant to be: unhurried, honest, unforgettable.

The Three Pillars

Our Specialties

Three timeless categories — refined, reimagined, and rooted in the flavors of the Mediterranean coast.

Wood-Fired Seafood

Line-caught branzino, octopus, and Mediterranean prawns, kissed by olive wood smoke and finished with lemon, herbs, and first-press oil.

Mezze & Small Plates

A procession of small, shareable dishes — whipped feta, marinated olives, saganaki, and heirloom tomatoes — the Aegean way of beginning a meal.

Cellar & Cocktail

A curated list of biodynamic Greek wines, amaro-forward cocktails, and rare Assyrtikos sourced directly from the volcanic soils of Santorini.
House Favorites

Our Favorites

Signature dishes chosen by our chefs and beloved by guests, available on both the dining and online menus.

Signature · Seafood

Whole Grilled Branzino

Mediterranean sea bass finished over olive wood with Castelvetrano olives, preserved lemon, and crushed fennel pollen.
From the Grill

Kalamata Lamb Skewers

Pasture-raised lamb marinated 48 hours in oregano, garlic, and yogurt — served with tzatziki and warm pita.
Small Plate

Flaming Saganaki

Sheep's milk kefalograviera, pan-seared and flambéed tableside with brandy, lemon, and cracked black pepper.
Raw Bar

Octopus Carpaccio

Paper-thin slices of slow-poached octopus, dressed in Kalamata oil, capers, and Meyer lemon — finished with micro basil.
Pasta & Grains

Saffron & Black Truffle Risotto

Carnaroli rice simmered in shellfish broth, finished with Persian saffron, Umbrian black truffle, and aged pecorino.
Dessert

Honey & Pistachio Baklava

Thirty-six hand-layered sheets of phyllo, Aegina pistachios, and wildflower honey — served warm with cardamom cream.
